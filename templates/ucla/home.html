The Online Language Archive at the University of Chicago, formerly the Digital Media Archive, holds some 2,100 language collections across 270 languages. This interface makes many of these collections freely available for use by researchers and members of speaker communities and lists many others that can be accessed by special arrangement.

Whether you are a linguist, anthropologist, a scholar looking for a global corpus of language in use, a student, language learner, a speaker of an endangered language, part of a heritage community with ties to a language, or a member of the general public, we hope you will find something here to explore.
